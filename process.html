Process

For this webpage, I wanted to go for a simple and neutral theme. I began with choosing the font and theme colors, I wanted a focus of black, white, grey, and used the primary and secondary buttons as pops of color! I was inspired by the GetBootstrap webpage and used that for most of my ideas. I also researched what fonts would look best by using Google Fonts and this article https://analyticsindiamag.com/tech-behind-facebooks-textstylebrush/.

Before beginning this webpage, I thought about who my niche would be, going forward when making decisions about how this webpage would look like, I thought about it through the niche's lens.

The niche would include employers, professors, peers, and maybe even friends or family. I wanted to make it seem interactive and fun yet had a professional look to it (also why I included the professional images), this is what made me choose neutral colors.

I have struggled with getting the webpage to look exactly how I visioned it to be, however I hope you like it!
